Performance guide 9–10 / Excellent
  • Exceptional understanding
  • Highly confident and articulate
  • Shows leadership qualities
  • Provides realistic and creative answers
7–8 / Very Good
  • Strong knowledge with minor weaknesses
  • Communicates clearly
  • Shows good judgment
5–6 / Satisfactory
  • Adequate knowledge
  • Some uncertainty or weak explanations
  • Potential for improvement
3–4 / Weak
  • Significant gaps in knowledge
  • Difficulty communicating ideas
  • Limited problem-solving ability
1–2 / Poor
  • Major lack of preparation
  • Unable to answer most questions effectively

For categories scored above /10, apply the same descriptors proportionally.
